Buffets & Catering

Freshly Made Sandwich & Cake Platters

Need food for a party, meeting, or get-together? We can help. All our buffet trays are freshly made to order in our kitchen, using the same quality ingredients we serve in the café every day. Whether it's sandwiches for a work do or cakes for a celebration, we keep it simple and delicious.

Everything is made by us, from scratch. Our sandwiches are prepared fresh with a variety of fillings on white or brown bread, and our cakes are baked right here. We've been doing this for years, and we know what works. Just give us a bit of notice, and we'll sort you out with exactly what you need.

Buffet trays start from £15, and we can cater for groups of any size. Whether you need a couple of trays or enough to feed a crowd, we're flexible. Give us a call on 07840 803555 to discuss what you're after, and we'll give you a price. It's that straightforward.

What We Offer

Sandwich Platters

Freshly made sandwiches with a selection of fillings including ham, cheese, tuna mayo, egg mayo, chicken mayo, and more. Available on white or brown bread, cut into halves or quarters.

Cake Platters

A selection of our homemade cakes and traybakes. Perfect for adding something sweet to your spread.

Mixed Platters

Can't decide? We can do a mix of sandwiches and cakes on the same tray.
